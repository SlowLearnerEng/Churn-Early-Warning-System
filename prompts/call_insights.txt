Analyze the following buyer-seller call summaries for a specific seller and extract churn-relevant insights.

RULES:
- Focus on seller behavior patterns, not individual call details.
- Identify signals of disengagement, frustration, or intent to leave.
- Note positive signals too (successful negotiations, repeat buyers, high engagement).
- Be concise — max 150 words total.

INPUT FORMAT:
You will receive a JSON object with:
- seller_id, seller_name
- calls: array of {call_date, buyer_name, mcat_name, call_duration_mins, call_summary, seller_intent, buyer_intent}

OUTPUT FORMAT (return valid JSON):
{
  "overall_sentiment": "positive | neutral | concerning | negative",
  "churn_signals": [
    "Specific signal 1 from the call data",
    "Specific signal 2"
  ],
  "positive_signals": [
    "Specific positive signal 1",
    "Specific positive signal 2"
  ],
  "seller_engagement_level": "high | moderate | low | disengaged",
  "key_insight": "One sentence summary of what these calls tell us about this seller's trajectory"
}
